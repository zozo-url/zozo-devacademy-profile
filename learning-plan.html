WEEK THREE: UPDATED GOALS/LEARNING PLAN

Handle stress/lethargy more productively:

  • Notice when I'm stressed/lethargic and check off what I could do to make it better
  • Look after my body: drink more water, eat well, take regular breaks, stretch, take deep breaths

Deal with code-related problems more effectively

  • Psuedocode
  • Google it
  • Ask for help more often

Priortise my time to mitigate stress and increase my ability to learn/engage:

  • Spend 1-2 hours after class recapping concepts and continuing to work on problems
  • Take an hour to reiterate particular concepts/problems that I need to reiterate
  • Limit extra curricular activities to an amount that I can feasibly handle
  • Give myself to time to reflect decompress each night through meditation and journaling
