MY LEARNING PLAN

STRENGTHS

  • Creative
  • Open-minded
  • Diplomatic
  • Driven

WEAKNESSES

Sometimes I get distracted, irritated and/or disillusioned, struggle to endure boring details/things I’m not interested in doing, get frustrated with people with ideas/approaches I don’t agree with.


HOW I'LL MANAGE MY WORKLOAD

PRODUCTIVITY

Being mindful of what external factors make distracted/irritated/disillusioned and structure my schedule accordingly to make the most of my time.

During foundations, I will prioritise getting up early and having a good morning routine with breakfast, yoga, journal writing, etc. (I will develop exactly what works through trial and error). I will also make sure I have lunch during the day and drink a lot of water. I will plan my weeks and my days out before I get started to keep me focused. I will work to take regular breaks and limit social media use.

During bootcamp, again I’ll make sure I’m eating well, drinking water and taking regular breaks. Staying mindful when approaching problems and follow the problem-solving process.

WORKING WITH OTHER LEARNERS/FACILITATORS

Maintain the practice of empathy, really listen to others suggestions and find positive compromises. Ultimately, work to build connections with those around me through humility, so asking/giving help is easy.

WORKING WITH INDUSTRY/COMMUNITY

Present myself with passion, integrity and humility, and take onboard their perspectives/insights.


HOW/WHERE I WILL SEEK HELP

I will try and work through the problem myself a couple of times with the help of internet searching, then ask other learners personally or through slack, then ask facilitators if I’m still stuck.


WHAT I EXPECT FROM FACILITATION TEAM

  • Humility
  • Support
  • Active interest
  • Meaningful critique

OTHER COMMITMENTS

Work on Friday/Saturday nights, gigs I’m playing/organizing, and other creative side projects.
